Sining na Mapagpalaya:
A Documentary Theater About Ericson Acosta

A Documentary Musical presented to Department of Performing Arts
Polytechnic University of the Philippines

Submitted by:
De Andres, Patricia Angelica Demetrio
Guevara, Eusebio III C.
Leonardo, Natahaniel L.
Mangi, Lianah Marie B.
Paigones, Jan Kimberly Mae T.
Puno, Ymanuel Luis M.

“Mula Kay Eric Hanggang” is a poignant artistic production that chronicles the life, struggle, and unwavering spirit of Filipino poet and activist Ericson Acosta. Through a powerful blend of performance and creative expression, the production amplifies his fight for national freedom, human rights, and justice—telling a compelling story of courage and advocacy.

Explore the Journey About the Kolektib

Welcome to the Journey of Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib Theatrical Production

This website serves as a detailed documentary through the essential stages of a live theatrical performance: Pre-Production, Production (including the live run), and Documentation & Archiving. For aspiring creatives, theater enthusiasts, students, or simply the curious, we present each stage with clarity and in-depth understanding.

Kalabaw sa Kubaw Kolektib aims to showcase the meticulous planning, collaborative efforts, and artistic dedication required to transform a script into a living, breathing stage act. Dive in to discover the passion behind the scenes!
